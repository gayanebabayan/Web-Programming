Seneca College

Information For Employers

Hire a Co-op Student

Co-operative (Co-op) Education programs give students the opportunity to integrate academic learning with paid practical work experience.
Only our top academic students are eligible for the co-op program. 
Co-op students are taught specific skills that can be given practical application in their work terms, making them an excellent year-round source 
of skilled, enthusiastic, flexible and productive employees who possess good communication skills, a positive attitude, and a desire to learn.

Hire Seneca Students and Graduates - Free Job Postings

Looking to hire students and graduates? Seneca’s Career Services offer a free job posting website, SenecaWorks, that provides you with the access 
to post your summer, part-time, full-time or contract job opportunities to more than 105,000 full-time and part-time Seneca students and grads.

Training Services

The Faculty of Continuing Education responds to requests for specialized or customized training in any of our program disciplines.
Organizations in virtually every sector of the economy rely on Seneca College’s Training Services to help build high-performance workforces.
Seneca Training Services provide advanced solutions that address strategic and operational priorities to powerfully impact your business 
performance, competitive advantage, profit margin and staff morale.
